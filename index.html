Our Story

Growing up in Small Town, Colorado, supporting local businesses wasn't just a trend, it was a way of life. My parents were both artists and entrepreneurs, so I saw firsthand how much heart and hard work goes into building something of your own. We relied on our community. Local markets, fairs, and events weren't just about shopping; they were about connection.

That sense of community and entrepreneurship is what inspired me to start Main Street Events. I want to create markets that bring people together in the same way, where local vendors can showcase their passion, shoppers can discover something unique, and the entire experience feels more like a celebration than just another shopping trip.

At Main Street Events, it's not just about what you buy, but the experience of finding it. We host markets in unique venues, showcasing incredible talent, serving craft drinks, and creating a fun and welcoming atmosphere. Our goal is to make sure every vendor has the opportunity to grow and every guest leaves with something special, whether it's a handcrafted piece, a great memory, or a new favorite brand to follow.

Our Mission

At Main Street Events, our mission is to create vibrant, high-energy markets that bring together local businesses, artisans, and the community. Our Goal

Our goal is to elevate the vendor market experience by hosting well-organized, well-promoted, and unforgettable events. We strive to provide small businesses with the best possible platform to grow while ensuring shoppers enjoy an exciting, one-of-a-kind market. From unique venues to intentionally selected vendors, we're committed to making every Main Street Event one that people look forward to and remember.
